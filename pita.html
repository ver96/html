Pita Pizza Recipe | 10 Minute Pizza Hack


This pita pizza recipe is the easiest and coolest way to make pizza!!





Ingredients:


  • ✔ Pita bread
  • ✔ Tomato sauce
  • ✔ Mozzarella cheese
  • ✔ Optional toppings

Directions:


1. Preheat oven to (400F | 200C).
2. Cut each pita bread and arrange on baking tray lined with parchment paper.
3. Spread 1-2 tablespoons of tomato sauce on each pita bread. Then sprinkle 40-50 grams of mozzarella cheese.
4. On top of each pizza place your favorite topping.
5. Bake for 10 minutes or until the cheese is melted.
