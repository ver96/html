Pizza Rolls Recipe


Easy homemade pizza rolls recipe.
These pizza rolls are so soft and flavorful.
You can fill them with topping that you like.





Ingredients:


  • ✔ 4 cups (500g) flour
  • ✔ 1 tablespoon instant-dry yeast
  • ✔ 1 tablespoon sugar
  • ✔ 1½ teaspoon salt
  • ✔ 1¼ cups (300 ml) water
  • ✔ 2 tablespoons (30g) melted butter
  • ✔ 13 oz (370g) tomato sauce
  • ✔ 13 oz (370g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ✔ 1 teaspoon oregano (?)
  • ✔ Olives/peperoni/extra cheese for topping
  • ✔ 1 egg for brushing

Directions:


1. In a bowl of a stand mixer, mix flour yeast and sugar. Add salt, melted butter and water and knead for 6-8 minutes.
2. Grease the bowl with oil. Place the dough in the bowl, cover with plastic wrap and let rise until doubled, about 1 hour.
3. On a floured work surface Roll the dough into rectangle.
4. Spread tomato sauce, crushed garlic and sprinkle oregano. Then sprinkle mozzarella and olives (optional). Leave about 2 cm uncovered on the edges.
5. gently roll the dough into a log. Slice into 12 equal pieces. Place the rolls in a baking dish lined with parchment paper. cover with plastic wrap and let rise 30 minutes.
6. Preheat oven to 360F (180C).
7. Brush the rolls with beaten egg. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until golden.
